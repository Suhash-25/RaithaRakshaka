📖 What is Prose?

📝

Prose is ordinary writing — the kind we use every day. It flows naturally like how we talk, read news, or tell stories.
NOT like poetry ✗

🗣️

Think of prose as normal sentences and paragraphs — no special rhythm or rhyme required!

⚡

Prose vs Poetry: Prose = natural flow of words. Poetry = rhythm & rhyme patterns.

💡 Key Concepts to Remember

Prose uses normal language — just like how we speak!

Stories are written in prose — novels, tales & adventures!

Prose helps us communicate ideas clearly and effectively.

No fixed rhyme or rhythm — prose flows freely!
